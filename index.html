About Kawasaki

Kawasaki Heavy Industries, Ltd. is a Japanese multinational corporation known for its high-performance motorcycles, aerospace technology, and heavy machinery. Founded in 1896, Kawasaki has a long history of engineering excellence and innovation.

The company entered the motorcycle industry in the 1960s and quickly became a global leader with its powerful and technologically advanced bikes. From the legendary Kawasaki Ninja series to the cutting-edge supercharged H2R, Kawasaki continues to push the boundaries of speed and performance.

With a commitment to quality and precision, Kawasaki motorcycles are designed for riders who crave adrenaline and perfection. Whether on the track or the open road, Kawasaki delivers an unparalleled riding experience.
